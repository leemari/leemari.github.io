About Me

Having graduated from Napier University Edinburgh in 1993 with a degree in computing, I have been developing software as a professional ever since. I currently work for E.ON IT UK Ltd., developing enterprise software bespoke to the Utility industry; this is a mixture of desktop / intranet and backend based solutions.

I began my professional career as a C programmer, I have been developing in Java since 1998 and C# .NET since 2002.

I have been doing a technical lead role for the past 12 years; designing solutions, reviewing code, mentoring, making technology decisions, coding etc.

I am interested in programming theory and the various programming paradigms. Whilst most of my experience has been with Imperative / Object Oriented paradigms, in the last few years I have come to realize the benefits to adopting the functional paradigm. I have considered numerous languages promoting the functional style of programming (Scala / Clojure / F#) and try to incorporate the style into my C# code.

For me, developing software is as much a hobby as a profession and, during 22 years in the profession, my enthusiasm for continued learning has never deteriorated.
